[Notes]

This is a linear regulator solution and is the simplest practical way to obtain 5.0 V from a 12.0 V DC input at a load current between 5 mA and 20 mA.
U1 should be a 5.0 V fixed regulator with guaranteed output tolerance within ±4% or better so the output remains inside 5.0 V ±0.2 V. The A-grade 7805 family is appropriate.
C1 should be placed close to U1 IN and C2 close to U1 OUT to maintain stability and reduce noise.
C3 is optional but recommended to improve transient load response and reduce output ripple if wiring is long or the load changes quickly.
Load power range is:
Pload(min) = 5.0 V × 5 mA = 25 mW
Pload(max) = 5.0 V × 20 mA = 100 mW
Regulator dissipation range is approximately:
Preg(min) = (12.0 V - 5.0 V) × 5 mA = 35 mW
Preg(max) = (12.0 V - 5.0 V) × 20 mA = 140 mW
Because the specified load current is always at least 5 mA, no additional dummy load resistor is required.
If the 12 V input is noisy or enters through long leads, an additional 100 nF ceramic capacitor may be placed in parallel with C1
