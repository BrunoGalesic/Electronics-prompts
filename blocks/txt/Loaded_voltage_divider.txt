
Circuit: 5.0V to 2.50V Loaded Divider
Source: Designed solution

[Components]

R2 = 1.00 kΩ, Package=Load, Function=Specified output load from VOUT to GND

[Hierarchy]

[Notes]
- R1 and the specified 1 kΩ load R2 form a 1:1 divider, so VOUT = 5.0 V × 1 kΩ / (1 kΩ + 1 kΩ) = 2.50 V nominal.
- Using R1 = 1.00 kΩ ±1% keeps VOUT within approximately 2.488 V to 2.513 V for an exact 1.00 kΩ load, which satisfies 2.50 V ±0.05 V.
- This solution depends on the 1 kΩ load being present; if the load is removed or changed significantly, VOUT will change.
