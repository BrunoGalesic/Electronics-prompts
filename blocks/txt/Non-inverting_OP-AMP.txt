[Notes]

This is a single analog stage implemented as a non-inverting op-amp amplifier.
The closed-loop gain is set by R1 and R2: Av = 1 + R1/R2 = 1 + 40k/10k = 5.
Therefore the transfer function is approximately VOUT = 5 × VIN.
For VIN = 0.20 V, the nominal output is VOUT = 1.00 V.
For VIN = 0.50 V, the nominal output is VOUT = 2.50 V.
Both required operating points are therefore satisfied within the ±0.05 V tolerance, assuming a suitable single-supply op-amp model and normal linear operation.
A +5 V single supply is suitable because the required output range is only 1.00 V to 2.50 V, well within the available supply range.
Net IN- was inferred as the inverting-input feedback node connecting U1(-IN), R1, and R2.
C1 is a local supply bypass capacitor for stable operation.
